# 3D / AI Video Prompts

Use these prompts for short generated loops, then export local `.mp4` or `.webm` assets for the portfolio. Keep all clips abstract: no brand logos, no readable secrets, no real IPs, no SSH prompts, no production dashboards, and no direct recreation of any reference site asset.

## Shared Direction

- Duration: 4-7 seconds, seamless loop.
- Color: off-black background, warm bone textural highlights, restrained orange scanning planes.
- Camera: slow dolly or slight parallax, no fast cuts.
- Texture: matte 3D surfaces, soft grain, high contrast, editorial tech mood.
- Negative prompt: cyberpunk city, neon overload, stock UI, readable code, readable credentials, real company logos, human faces, cartoon, glossy plastic, decorative glowing spheres.

## Export Notes

- Export at `1920px` wide minimum for hero, `1280px` wide minimum for card clips.
- Use muted audio or no audio.
- Keep contrast high enough that dark details remain visible after browser compression.
- Save clips under `static/media/` if they are later wired into the SvelteKit app.
